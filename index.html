About Us

Bagi Boga is a student-led food surplus redistribution for stunting prevention through the role of front-line organization. Bagi Boga focuses on preventing stunting and synergizing with the health sector, namely the puskesmas and posyandu as a movement that intensively encourages better and adequate nutrition for mothers and babies who are at risk of stunting. With the support of cadres and the health sector, Bagi Boga prevents food waste by distributing surplus good quality food products that are at risk of being wasted or destroyed because they are not sold.
